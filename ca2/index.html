How To Play

  1. the player moves around the screen with the arrow keys
  2. the enemys with the scythe can kill you if the touch you
  3. the enemys without the scythes can disarm you and prevent you from casting spells
  4. if you are dissarmed you will see a blue rectangle close to your player and you will not be able to cast spells
  5. press e to disarm your opponents
  6. press d to sheild yourself from dissarm
  7. press a to dash very fast
  8. press f to fire a killing spell
  9. you pogress by killing the enemys
